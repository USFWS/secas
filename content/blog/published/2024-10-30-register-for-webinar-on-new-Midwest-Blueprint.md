[Register at this link](https://us02web.zoom.us/meeting/register/tZUlceyuqDkvGNHjXJuvIerhsbLWSZz097r3) to participate on Thursday, November 21st at 1 pm ET. You'll also be able to read more about it in next month's newsletter!

Yes, that's the same date as [the webinar about version 2024 of the Southeast Conservation Blueprint](https://calendar.google.com/calendar/u/0/event?eid=NmVnOGY3YTlpYmNxN2RrYzgxbjgyYWtkb2Ugc2VjYXNzb3V0aGVhc3RAbQ), which will be held earlier that morning at 10 am ET--it's a Blueprint double-header! We hope you're able to attend both.
