These short videos aren't new, but I ran across them again in looking up some deep-sea related information. It's so great to be able to see the amazing deep-sea diversity in the Southeast. I'm also thankful that NOAA efforts like the [Okeanos Explorer](https://oceanexplorer.noaa.gov/okeanos/welcome.html), which make videos and share images to make this world more accessible to all of us.
