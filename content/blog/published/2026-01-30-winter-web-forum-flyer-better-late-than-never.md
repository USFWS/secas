The partial government shutdown last fall shook up the SECAS web forum schedule, and staff had to make some adjustments. So this flyer for the December, January, and February webinar series is coming out quite late--but hopefully better late than never!

SECAS hosts this web forum on the third Thursday of every month at 10 am Eastern time. [»Click here for an interactive pdf of the flyer with links to register](/pdf/ThirdThursdayWebForumFlyer_Winter2025_sm.pdf).

<!--more-->

- **December 11th, 2025 @ 10 am ET:** _Improvements in Southeast Conservation Blueprint 2025 from the continent to the Caribbean._ On a rare second Thursday webinar in December, SECAS Science Coordinator Rua Mordecai shared an update on the latest improvements to the Southeast Blueprint. If you missed it and want to get caught up, the recording and a pdf of the powerpoint slides are linked from the registration page: [https://events.gcc.teams.microsoft.com/event/4ebdcc29-7124-4246-ae0b-ab99d32fbb0f@0693b5ba-4b18-4d7b-9341-f32f400a5494](https://events.gcc.teams.microsoft.com/event/4ebdcc29-7124-4246-ae0b-ab99d32fbb0f@0693b5ba-4b18-4d7b-9341-f32f400a5494).

- **January 15th, 2026 @ 10 am ET:** _An under the hood peek of Grasslandia, SGI's forthcoming "all Things Southeast grasslands" website._ In January, a team of three speakers from the Southeast Grasslands Institute (SGI) gave a pre-release tour of "Grasslandia", reviewing the types of content they're planning to release and asking for feedback on their interactive maps. To see the slides and recording, follow the links from the registration page: [https://events.gcc.teams.microsoft.com/event/af4e240b-9322-4e65-a71b-47f5e22bef15@0693b5ba-4b18-4d7b-9341-f32f400a5494](https://events.gcc.teams.microsoft.com/event/af4e240b-9322-4e65-a71b-47f5e22bef15@0693b5ba-4b18-4d7b-9341-f32f400a5494).

- **February 19th, 2026 @ 10 am ET:** _Spotlight on grassland and savanna restoration across multiple states._ This web forum will feature three speakers from three different state fish and wildlife agencies spotlighting grassland and savanna conservation and restoration efforts across different parts of the Southeast. The final speaker lineup is still being confirmed, but tentatively will represent Georgia, Missouri, and Mississippi. Register here: [https://events.gcc.teams.microsoft.com/event/57a74516-cf1c-45ad-8231-b76957d7d7ff@0693b5ba-4b18-4d7b-9341-f32f400a5494](https://events.gcc.teams.microsoft.com/event/57a74516-cf1c-45ad-8231-b76957d7d7ff@0693b5ba-4b18-4d7b-9341-f32f400a5494).
