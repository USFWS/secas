[Register at this link](https://us02web.zoom.us/meeting/register/tZUlceyuqDkvGNHjXJuvIerhsbLWSZz097r3) to participate on November 21st at 1 pm ET. You'll also be able to read more about it in next month's newsletter!<!--more-->

Yes, that's the same date as the webinar about the latest Southeast Conservation Blueprint, which will be earlier that morning at 10 am ET--it's a Blueprint double-header! We hope you're able to attend both.
