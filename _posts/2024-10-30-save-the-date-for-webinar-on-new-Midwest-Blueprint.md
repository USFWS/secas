Our colleagues with the [Midwest Landscape Initiative](https://www.mlimidwest.org/) are releasing the next version of the [Midwest Conservation Blueprint](https://mcap-fws.hub.arcgis.com/pages/midwest-conservation-blueprint) in a webinar next month. 

[Register at this link](https://us02web.zoom.us/meeting/register/tZUlceyuqDkvGNHjXJuvIerhsbLWSZz097r3) to participate on November 21st at 1 pm ET. 

Yes, that's the same date as the webinar about the latest Southeast Conservation Blueprint, which will be earlier that morning at 10 am ET--it's a Blueprint double-header! We hope you're able to attend both.
